About Me

My name is Riley Tucker. I am 27 years old. I was born in Lansing, MI on November 17th, 1994 at approximately 8am. I am a 5th year Computer Science major at Michigan State University taking a cognate in Media and Information. I first decided I wanted to pursue a career in computer science around 2016 after unfortunate life circumstances forced me to consider my future more seriously. I had always had an interest in becoming a game developer, but had often heard that those positions can be pretty nightmarish when first starting out, so I decided I would obtain the skillset to become a software developer and build my portfolio of work so that I could have an advantageous position when attempting to enter the game dev industry, instead of attempting to enter as a college graduate begging for a job in a field in which I have no experience. Some games that inspire me to wish become a game developer include:

  • The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild
  • Hollow Knight
  • Stardew Valley
  • Forager
  • Littlewood
  • Terraria
  • Risk of Rain

I also play guitar and do vocals in a metal band and have several hobbies focused around music including:

  • Playing Guitar
  • Doing Vocals
  • Writing Music
  • Performing with my Band, Son of Scylla

I have many dreams and aspirations and the past 5 years have been a struggle to determine in what I wish to specialize and excel. Sadly, though music is my passion, I have mostly ruled it out as a career choice and now consider it more as a secondary skill that can be used to further my aspirations to be a game developer. The income from music, especially in my genre of choice (metal) is fairly inconsistent/unpredictable and unlikely to ever be adequate and even less likely to be excessive.
